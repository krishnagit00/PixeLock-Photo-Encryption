How Pixelock Works
What We Collect

We store your encrypted file and minimal metadata (filename, upload time) to facilitate the transfer. We do not require personal accounts or verify identities for basic transfers.

Automatic Deletion

Data hygiene is our priority. Files are automatically permanently deleted from our servers 24 hours after upload. Once deleted, they cannot be recovered by anyone, including us.

Fernet Encryption

Files are encrypted using the Fernet (AES-128) standard before they are saved to our disk. Even if our physical server is compromised, your files remain unreadable gibberish.

Session-Only History

Your "Recent Transfers" history is stored in your browser's temporary session. We do not keep a permanent log of who sent what to whom. When you close your browser, the trail vanishes.

Special Features & Lockers

Smart Lockers: Our personal storage uses PBKDF2 hashing to secure your PINs. We never see your raw PIN. Anonymous Feedback: Our feedback system is designed to be optional and discreet, allowing you to help us improve without doxxing yourself.
